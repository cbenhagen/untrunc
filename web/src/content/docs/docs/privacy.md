## Local processing

**rsv.repair** runs entirely **in your browser**. Your reference clip, `.rsv` file, and output video are read and written **on your machine**. We do **not** upload your media to our servers as part of the repair flow.

## Support chat (optional)

If you use **Intercom** support chat, that service may process messages and technical data under [their policies](https://www.intercom.com/legal/privacy). You can accept or decline chat cookies when prompted.
